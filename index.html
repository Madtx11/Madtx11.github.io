Madisons' Chocolate Nutmeg Oatmeal

Time: apr. 10-15 min.

List of Ingreadents:

  • Nutmeg-1/2 tsp
  • Cinnamon- 1 tsp
  • Insant Oatmeal Quackers
  • Brown Sugar 1/4 cup
  • Coca Powder
  • (Optional) Fruit

(Note: this is for a small serving aprx. 2 or 3.) First, bring the water to a boil, once the water is boiled, add oatmeal. Turn it low, after it is how you like it, add all the ingdents. Enjoy!
